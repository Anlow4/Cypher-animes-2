Do you know something about anime?

Anime is a term that defines cartoons of Japanese origin and also the elements related to these cartoons. In Japan, anime refers to animation in general.

The topics covered in anime are very varied (dramas, fiction, horror, adventure, psychology, romance, behavior, mythology, etc.). Another important characteristic of current anime is the occurrence of technological elements in the plots of the stories.

Naruto

1st Best anime

Naruto is an anime based on the manga of the same name written by Masashi Kishimoto. The series revolves around the adventures experienced by Naruto Uzumaki, a young orphan inhabitant of the Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the Fifth Hokage, the greatest warrior and ruler of the village.

One Piece

2nd Best anime

The series focuses on Monkey D. Luffy, a young man made of rubber, who, inspired by his childhood idol, the mighty pirate Shanks the Red, sets out on a journey across the East Blue Sea to find the mythical treasure, the One Piece. , and proclaim himself the Pirate King.

Dragon ball Z

3rd Best anime

the franchise tells the story of Son Goku, a warrior who discovers that he is part of a legacy of powerful alien conquerors - and starts to defend his adopted planet, Earth, from other equally super strong beings capable of extraordinary feats.
